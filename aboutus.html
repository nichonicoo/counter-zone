ABOUT-ZONE

Welcome to "Counter-Zone"! We are a passionate and dedicated development team committed to delivering an exciting and thrilling gaming experience. With a combination of expertise in game design, graphics, and storytelling, we have created a captivating world for you to explore. In "Counter-Zone," you will enter intense battlegrounds where your tactics and combat skills will be put to the extreme test. We are dedicated to providing exhilarating action and expansive adventure maps with captivating battle narratives. We value your feedback, and we hope you enjoy the experience we have crafted. Get ready to be part of an epic battle in "Counter-Zone" and showcase your combat prowess!

"Counter-Zone" is the brainchild of three talented individuals: Ilham Syahrul Ramadhan, Nicholas Terrence, and Samuel Theopylus Wieguna. With a shared passion for gaming and a vision for creating an immersive and engaging experience, they came together to bring this game to life. Ilham Syahrul Ramadhan, with his expertise in game mechanics and level design, ensures that every moment in "Counter-Zone" is filled with excitement and strategic depth. Nicholas Terrence, a master of graphics and visual effects, crafts stunning visuals that bring the game world to life, captivating players with their attention to detail. Samuel Theopylus Wieguna, the storyteller of the team, weaves gripping narratives that immerse players in the rich lore of "Counter-Zone."
